DataTAAG Virtualization solution offers complete virtualization management for servers and desktops on the same infrastructure. Built on Red Hat Enterprise Linux® or VMware, DataTAAG delivers reliable business agility and continuity for critical Linux and Microsoft Windows workloads at the scale you need.

DataTAAG is a VMware & Red Hat Enterprise partner with specialists in every field of virtualization. DataTAAG prides itself in truly understanding and delivering virtualization solutions, as VMware and Red Hat intended. With 5 top qualified consultants and engineers in Virtualization technologies, we are well positioned to visit your business, understand your needs and design a solution that will address those needs, whilst saving you time and money, and reducing your carbon footprint.

With green technology initiatives a growing requirement, VMware & Red Hat allow DataTAAG to deliver a leading edge in providing innovative technology solutions that often earn our clients ISO standards in energy efficiency, and in some case winning our clients green awards.

Virtualization can vastly improve efficiency, free up resources, and cut costs. DataTAAG ensures our clients, by virtualizing their IT infrastructure they will not have to sacrifice performance, security, and existing investments. As you plan for the cloud, you need to be able to choose your technology and scale for the future- without more vendor lock-in.

B) Enterprise Performance Assurance

A reactive, manual, and fragmented approach to performance is no longer sufficient for today’s dynamic IT environments. DataTAAG delivers pre-emptive, automated and integrated performance assurance for business critical environments using leading Performance Management Tools.

DataTAAG helps you quickly diagnose and resolve performance bottlenecks and right-size your infrastructure to deliver on the promise of exceptional performance, user productivity, and ROI.

When a user calls and complains about service performance, it is often difficult and very time consuming to pinpoint the root cause of the issue. Is it Citrix, VMware, VDI, is it the Java application, database server, or storage, is it the network or is there a issue in the cloud? By correlating and visualizing all performance drivers and dependencies - from desktops and applications to servers, network and storage.

DataTAAG helps you proactively discover, instantly diagnose, and rapidly resolve even the most challenging performance issues before they impact user experience and the success of your transformational IT initiatives.

C) Enterprise Database Virtualization

Certain gaps threaten to derail the notion of a supremely automated IT infrastructure. One of these is a convenient, secure way to leverage the highest-value information owned by a company: typically, its customer and product databases.

In most companies, databases get copied more often than you may realize. Developers need database copies for development and testing, and with internal application development operations of any size, that may mean several copies. Plus, you need database copies for offline, processing-intensive applications such as business intelligence to avoid slowing the production database to a crawl.

DataTAAG provides a database virtualization platform that enables customers to create virtual copies that require a fraction of the storage space and human labor that full, conventional copies on demand.
